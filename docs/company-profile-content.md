# About Us
Heritage Jute Fibers is a Bangladesh-based trading company with over 15 years of industry experience, specializing in sustainable and high-quality jute solutions for global markets. We are committed to delivering eco-friendly products that meet international quality standards while supporting a greener future.

Our product portfolio includes Jute Sacks & Bags, Jute Yarn & Twine, Jute Cloth, Jute Rope, Jute Sliver, and Cut Jute Fiber—serving a wide range of industrial and packaging needs.

Backed by strong sourcing capability and product knowledge, we ensure reliable supply, consistent quality, and flexible customization options for both bulk orders and specific requirements. Our focus is to provide durable, sustainable packaging solutions that help protect and preserve your goods effectively.

We would be pleased to explore how our jute products can add value to your business and support your sourcing needs. Quotations are available upon request.

## Sacking Sacks

Sacking sacks are bags made from coarse jute sacking fabric, commonly used for packing
and transporting bulk goods like grains, sugar, and agricultural products due to their
strength and breathability.
Quality: Btwill, Gunny, DW (Double Wrap)
Neight: 600 grams and above
Package: Each bale contains 300 or 400 bags, pressed using a hydraulic press and
secured with iron belts.
MOO: 1 × 20' FCL or 1 × 40' FCL.

## Jute Sliver

Jute sliver is a loose, untwisted bundle of parallel jute fibres produced during the early
spinning process, used as the intermediate material before drawing and yarn making.

Quality: Tossa, Mesta
Weight: 14+ kg per roll
Package: 70+ rolls, ~1000 kg per pallet, PP covered.
MOQ: 1x40' FCL = 20 - 22 Mtons

## Cut Jute Fiber

Cut jute fibers are jute fibres cut into short, uniform lengths (instead of long strands),
mainly used for stuffing, insulation, non-woven products, and industrial applications.

Quality: Tossa, Mesta (Natural White Jute)
Cut Length: 2 Cm to 20 Cm
Package: Each bale 130kg, pressed using a hydraulic press and secured with iron belts.
MOQ: 1 × 20' FCL = 13 Mtons or 1 x 40' FCL = 26 Mtons
