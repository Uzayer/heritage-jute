## Applications

Jute yarn is one of the most widely used natural fibers globally, second only to cotton. Its strength, flexibility, and sustainability make it an essential material in multiple industries:

- Weaving burlap fabric and Hessian Yarn for sacks and bags

- Burlap yarn for rustic crafts and packaging

- Carpet yarn for backing and finishing

- Fine yarn of jute for blending with other fibers in apparel

- Twine, ropes, and decorative crafts


Whether you need yarn jute for heavy-duty sacking or fine textiles, we supply consistent quality with every order.

### **Types of Jute Yarn**

We supply various types of jute yarn to match different applications:

- **White/Mesta Jute Yarn:** Brighter color, traditional uses

- **Tossa Jute Yarn:** Soft, long, silky, stronger fibers, brownish golden shade

- **Hessian Yarn:** For fine burlap and light fabric

- **Sacking Yarn:** For heavy-duty fabrics

- **Carpet Yarn:** Processed for the carpet industry

- **Fine Yarns:** For delicate textiles and apparel blends


Our jute yarn can be natural, bleached, dyed, or custom-processed to your exact requirements.
