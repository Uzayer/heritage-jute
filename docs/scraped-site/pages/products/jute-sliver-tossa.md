## Applications

#### Jute Sliver

**Weight:** 12/13 kg per roll

**Packing:** Rope-tied, Hessian-wrapped, or palletized for safe export handling

Long fiber slivers provide strength and reduce breakage, ideal for fine-count yarn manufacturing.

- **Nonwoven & Paper Industry (Kenaf/Mesta Blends):**


Lightweight, cost-effective fiber blend for **agricultural mats, erosion control, and reinforced jute sheets**.

Used for **jute ropes, twines, and coarse fabrics**, particularly suited for heavy-duty fiber applications.

Widely used in **road construction, slope stabilization, and drainage projects** due to its water-permeable yet durable nature.

Enhances tensile strength in **natural and recycled paper production**.

Integrated in **automotive panels, construction boards, and acoustic materials** for sustainable industries.

- **Furniture & Handicrafts:**


Used as **stuffing, padding, and eco-craft material** for furniture and traditional handmade goods.
