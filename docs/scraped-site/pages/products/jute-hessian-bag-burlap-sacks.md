## Applications

Our **Jute Hessian Bags** and **jute burlap sacks** are trusted by farmers, traders, and exporters worldwide for their safe and eco-friendly packaging. Their natural ventilation keeps contents fresh and dry, while the strong jute weave provides reliable protection during storage and shipping.

**Ideal for Packing, Storing, and Exporting:**

- **Grains & Cereals:** Rice, wheat, barley, maize, corn, pulses, lentils, chickpeas

- **Vegetables:** Potatoes, onions, garlic, ginger, sweet potatoes, yams

- **Cash Crops:** Coffee beans, cocoa beans, tea leaves, nuts

- **Spices & Herbs:** Turmeric, chili, dry herbs, cardamom, cloves

- **Animal Feed:** Bulk feed and fodder for livestock

- **Industrial Use:** Sandbags, erosion control, flood barriers


**These jute hessian bags are widely used in:**

- **Agriculture:** Safe storage for harvested crops

- **Food Exports:** Meeting EU, USA, and global food-grade packaging requirements

- **Retail & Wholesale Markets:** Bulk packaging for farmers, cooperatives, and distributors

- **Emergency & Environmental:** Sandbags for flood defense and soil erosion control


### **Available Bag Types:**

- **MOT (Mineral Oil Treated)—Economical** and moisture-resistant
- **VOT (Vegetable Oil Treated)—Food**-grade, eco-friendly finish preferred in **EU** and **USA** markets
- **HCF (Hydrocarbon Free)—Premium** treatment for organic and high-value agricultural exports; fully compliant with **European food safety standards**
