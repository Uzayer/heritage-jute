# Jute Cloth

**Jute Cloth & Fabric – Hessian, Burlap, and Industrial Applications**

At Heritage Jute Fibers, we specialize in exporting high-quality **jute cloth** and **jute fabric** to global markets. Known for its durability and eco-friendliness, jute is one of the world’s most sustainable fibers. Our collection includes **hessian cloth, burlap cloth, and jute carpet backing cloth**, widely used across industries for packaging, agriculture, textiles, and home décor.

**Types of Jute Cloth We Supply**

- **Hessian Cloth (Burlap Cloth)** – A versatile fabric used for packaging, agriculture, and construction. Known for its breathability and eco-friendly nature.
- **Jute Fabric Rolls** – Available in different widths and patterns for industrial and decorative use.
- **Jute Carpet Backing Cloth (CBC)** – Widely used in the carpet industry as a natural and strong backing material.
- **Printed Jute Fabric** – Customized with logos, designs, or patterns for branding, retail, and creative applications.
- **Raw Jute Fiber & Processed Cloth** – Exported for spinning, weaving, and manufacturing across industries.

**Benefits of Burlap and Hessian Cloth**

- **Eco-Friendly Alternative to Plastic** – 100% biodegradable and sustainable.
- **Durable & Strong** – Withstands heavy use in agriculture, packaging, and construction.
- **Breathable Fabric** – Ideal for crops, coffee, and commodities that require ventilation.
- **Customizable Designs** – Can be printed or patterned for branding and décor.
- **Cost-Effective** – Affordable compared to synthetic alternatives.

**Uses of Hessian and Jute Cloth**

- **Agriculture**: Sacks, seed storage, and crop protection.
- **Industrial Packaging**: Burlap cloth for coffee beans, rice, and bulk goods.
- **Carpet Industry**: Jute carpet backing cloth is used worldwide.
- **Construction**: Hessian fabric is applied for curing concrete and erosion control.
- **Retail & Branding**: Printed jute cloth and bags for eco-friendly promotions.
- **Home & Décor**: Rustic jute fabric for crafts, furniture, and interior designs.
