# Jute Products

At Heritage Jute Fibers, we proudly export a wide range of **jute products** to global markets. As a reliable **supplier of jute and jute goods**, we specialize in bulk and wholesale solutions designed for agriculture, packaging, textiles, and industrial applications. Our expertise in sustainable manufacturing ensures that every **product of jute** we deliver meets international quality standards.

## **Our Export Range of Jute and Jute Products**

- **Jute Yarn** – Export-quality yarn for weaving, carpet backing, and textile industries.
- **Jute Sliver** – Semi-processed jute fiber supplied to spinners and manufacturers.
- **Jute Rugs** – Natural and eco-friendly floor coverings for wholesale buyers.
- **Jute Ropes** – Strong ropes for shipping, agriculture, and heavy-duty applications.
- **Jute Hessian Bags** – Widely used for agricultural exports, food storage, and bulk commodities.
- **Jute Cloth** – Durable fabric for packaging, crafts, and industrial supply.
- **Hessian Cloth (Burlap Cloth)** – Exported for agriculture, upholstery, and construction use.
- **Jute Bags (Customized & Printed)** – Wholesale supply of eco-friendly and **personalised jute bags** for branding, coffee trade, and promotions.

## **Why Import Jute Goods from Us?**

- **Global Export Expertise** – Serving businesses across multiple countries.
- **Wholesale & Bulk Supply** – Cost-effective solutions for large-scale buyers.
- **Eco-Friendly Alternative to Plastic** – 100% natural, biodegradable, and sustainable.
- **Customised Solutions** – Printed, painted, and branded jute products available for bulk orders.
- **Trusted by Coffee & Commodity Exporters** – Our **burlap bags** and **jute rice bags** are widely used for international shipments.

## **Applications of Exported Jute Products**

- **Agriculture & Commodities**: Jute packaging bags, rice sacks, and burlap coffee bags.
- **Industrial Use**: Jute yarn, ropes, and cut fibers for global manufacturing.
- **Textiles & Décor**: Rugs, cloth, and hessian supplied to wholesale buyers.
- **Corporate Branding**: Bulk **customised jute bags** for promotions and trade shows.
